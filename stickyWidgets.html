Sticky position widgets

小部件可以移动到网格上的精确位置，而网格不会试图将其缩小到更小的大小。同样，小部件也不会自动离开，只会在鼠标上移动 Widgets can be moved to an exact postion on the grid and the grid will not attempt to collapse it down to a smaller size. Also widgets will not move out of the way automatically, but will move only on mouse up
